# Summarizing Whiteboards, Receipts, Diagrams, Slides, and Handwritten Notes

Ever snapped a photo of a whiteboard, then never looked at it again? Of course you did. That photo is now living in your camera roll beside a blurry picture of your lunch and a screenshot you meant to send three weeks ago.

The good news: ChatGPT can help turn messy visual notes into something useful.

## Why this matters

A lot of important information lives in ugly formats. Whiteboards get erased. Receipts pile up. Slides are too wordy. Handwritten notes can look like ancient treasure maps.

Instead of retyping everything yourself, you can upload an image and ask ChatGPT to summarize it, extract key points, organize it, or turn it into a cleaner version you can actually use.

This saves time and helps you do something with the information, not just store it.

## The idea in plain English

ChatGPT can look at an image and pull out the useful content from it. That might mean:

- summarizing a whiteboard brainstorm
- extracting totals and dates from a receipt
- explaining a diagram in simple language
- turning slide photos into study notes
- cleaning up handwritten notes into bullet points

Think of it like giving your visual mess a translator and an organizer.

The trick is to tell ChatGPT what kind of result you want. "What is this?" is okay. "Summarize this whiteboard into action items" is much better.

## Simple example

Let’s say you upload a photo of a whiteboard from a meeting.

You could prompt:

> Summarize this whiteboard into:
> - main ideas
> - decisions made
> - action items
> - open questions  
> If any handwriting is unclear, tell me which parts are uncertain.

That last line matters. If the image is messy, you want ChatGPT to flag uncertain text instead of confidently making things up like a guy in a meeting who definitely did not read the memo.

Here are a few other useful prompt patterns:

| Image type | Prompt idea |
|---|---|
| Receipt | Extract the store name, date, total, tax, and items if readable. Then give me a one-line summary. |
| Diagram | Explain this diagram in plain English for a beginner. |
| Slides | Turn these slides into concise study notes with headings and bullet points. |
| Handwritten notes | Clean up these notes into organized bullets. Mark anything unclear. |
| Whiteboard | Convert this brainstorm into categories, next steps, and a short summary. |

## Quick tips

- **Say what you want extracted.** Dates, totals, action items, definitions, decisions, and questions are all fair game.
- **Ask it to mark unclear text.** This is one of the smartest things you can do.
- **Use photos with decent lighting.** ChatGPT is helpful, not magical.
- **Ask for a format.** Try bullets, a table, a checklist, or a clean summary.
- **Break big jobs into steps.** First extract the text, then summarize it.
- **Double-check important details.** Especially with receipts, numbers, names, and handwritten content.

## Quick summary

ChatGPT can turn photos of whiteboards, receipts, diagrams, slides, and handwritten notes into useful summaries and organized text. The key is to be specific about what you want and to ask it to flag anything unclear. Messy image in, cleaner thinking out.
